# Vision

## What this product is
Marge is an AI pastoral assistant for churches. It helps pastors and church staff turn scattered care responsibilities into a reliable daily workflow.

## Who it serves
- pastors with too many follow-ups to remember manually
- executive pastors and pastoral admins managing care load
- churches that need warm, practical ministry follow-through without heavyweight church software complexity

## Core promise
Marge helps church leaders know who needs care today, draft outreach in their voice, track care situations, and keep follow-up moving so fewer people fall through the cracks.

## Current constraints
- the product handles sensitive pastoral and care data
- current momentum is strongest in internal pilot/staging use, not broad public release
- operational polish and onboarding clarity still matter as much as feature depth right now

## Non-goals
- becoming a generic church CRM
- replacing pastoral judgment with automated decisions
- optimizing for flashy AI novelty over practical ministry usefulness

## What success looks like
- pastors use Marge in a real daily/weekly ministry rhythm
- care follow-through improves and is visible
- pilot churches see Marge as a trusted workflow assistant, not a toy demo
- the product becomes credible to sell and support as a real church operations tool
