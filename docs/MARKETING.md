# Marketing

## ICP
- pastors and church staff who feel constant care-follow-up pressure
- churches that need consistency in pastoral care without adding staff headcount
- churches that value warmth and practical ministry usefulness over enterprise complexity

## Positioning
Marge is a warm AI pastoral assistant that helps pastors know who needs care today, follow up consistently, and keep ministry relationships from slipping.

## Main pain points
- too many people to remember manually
- inconsistent follow-up on visitors and care situations
- pastoral admin burden spread across notes, memory, and scattered systems
- missed moments of care because no reliable daily workflow exists

## Channel ideas
- founder-led demos into church/operator networks
- direct pastor/executive-pastor outreach with clear workflow examples
- landing page and waitlist capture
- pastoral operations content explaining care workflow pain and relief
- targeted pilot offers for churches with visible care/admin strain

## Content angles
- who needs care today should not live only in a pastor’s head
- pastoral follow-up without sounding robotic or corporate
- why churches need a care workflow assistant, not just another database
- a morning assistant for ministry follow-through

## Current experiments
- tighten workflow-first demo narrative from staging reality
- turn Marge’s strongest current workflows into pilot sales assets
- align landing and sales copy with the live product’s best proven use cases
