# Marge Privacy Policy

Defines how Marge handles sensitive church and pastoral information.

## Privacy classes

### Public
Can appear in prayer bulletin or broad church-facing output.
Examples:
- general prayer request approved for sharing
- birthdays and anniversaries
- non-sensitive announcements

### Private
Pastor-visible by default.
Examples:
- personal prayer request not approved for public sharing
- absence concerns
- routine pastoral notes
- visitor notes with family context

### Sensitive
Restricted pastoral data.
Examples:
- counseling-related notes
- crisis or family-conflict details
- medical details beyond what is needed for care
- financial hardship details
- anything the pastor marks as especially confidential

## Rules

- sensitive data never appears in public-facing outputs
- private prayer requests are never auto-promoted to public
- counseling content should not be summarized loosely or inferred beyond facts entered
- MCP and chat interfaces should honor the same privacy rules as the web app
- when in doubt, keep visibility narrow

## Product behavior

Marge should prefer:
- enough detail to help the pastor care well
- not so much detail that sensitive information is spread casually

Warmth is not permission to overshare.
